Dahibara

  • Soak Urad Dal: Soak whole urad dal (split black gram) in water for 2-4 hours.
  • Grind Batter: Grind the soaked dal into a smooth batter with minimal water. Aim for a thick consistency.
  • Seasoning: Add salt and a pinch of hing (asafoetida) to the batter.

Dalma

  • Choose Your Dal: Select your preferred lentils like toor dal (pigeon pea), moong dal (mung bean), or chana dal (split chickpea). Wash and soak for 3-4 hours.
  • Pressure Cook Dal and Vegetables (Optional): Add soaked dal, vegetables (if using), turmeric powder, salt, and water to a pressure cooker. Cook for 2-3 whistles or until the dal is soft.
  • Temper the Dal: Heat oil in a separate pan. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and dry red chilies for tempering (tadka).

Pakhala

  • Soak Rice: Rinse and soak basmati rice for at least 30 minutes.
  • Marinate Meat/Vegetables (Optional): If using meat (chicken, lamb), marinate with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices (red chili powder, coriander powder, etc.) for at least 30 minutes. You can also use vegetables for a vegetarian version.
  • Prepare Caramelized Onions (Optional): Fry sliced onions with ghee/oil until golden brown. Set aside for layering. (This adds richness and flavor)
  • Cook Rice: In a separate pot, boil rice with spices like cloves, cardamom, and bay leaf. Drain when partially cooked.

Biryani

  • Cook Rice: Prepare plain white rice by boiling it until slightly undercooked.
  • Prepare Souring Agent: Use curd (yogurt) or buttermilk. Ensure it's slightly sour for best results.
  • Water and Seasoning: Add water to the cooled rice in a bowl. Adjust the water quantity to achieve your desired consistency (thinner or thicker). Season with salt.
